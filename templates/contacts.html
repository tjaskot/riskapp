PROJECT SPONSOR / Team


ODASA-CEs Army decision-makers with cost, performance and economic analysis in the form of expertise, models, data, estimates, and analysis. The ODASA-CE Networks, Information Systems, Software and Electronics Costing (NISEC) Division is the lead for developing life cycle cost estimates for communications-electronics systems, major automated information systems (AIS), defense business systems (DBS) and software intensive systems. NISEC is stationed at Fort Belvoir, VA, and is the government lead commissioning for this study thru George Mason University (GMU) Volgenau School of Engineering.
